Welcome to DeafHack!

Jun 12, 2013 by William Albright { #general }

Hi! This is more/less a formality, a site launch post. There's much not to see here yet, but hopefully as the freenode IRC channel #deafhack grows, the content here grows too!

The goal of DeafHack is to give the technical crowd within the deaf community a place to discuss their ideas, aspirations, and projects, a la Hacker News. Eventually, I hope we will be contributing open source code as a group.

Check back here every once and then for updates!
